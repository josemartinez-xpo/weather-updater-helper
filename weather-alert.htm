              Update as of 12/13/19 1:30 PM ET

              Please review the following when planning your LTL shipments surrounding the holidays:

              LTL operations in the United States and Puerto Rico will be closed the following days: Tuesday, December 24 ; Wednesday, December 25.

              • Freight picked up in the U.S. or Puerto Rico on December 23 will be held until December 26 when normal operations resume. There will be no pickups or deliveries in the U.S. or Puerto Rico on December 24 or December 25
              • Limited Linehaul: December 26, 27, 30

              LTL operations in Canada will be closed on the following days: Tuesday, December 24 ; Wednesday, December 25 ; Thursday, December 26.

              • Freight picked up in Canada on December 23 will be held until December 27 when normal operations resume. There will be no pickups or deliveries in Canada on December 24, December 25, or December 26.
              • Limited Linehaul: December 27, 30

              LTL operations in the U.S., Puerto Rico and Canada will be closed on Wednesday, January 1.

              • Freight picked up in the U.S., Puerto Rico or Canada on December 31 will be held until January 2 when normal operations resume. There will be no pickups or deliveries on January 1.

              Thank you for your patience and understanding. We wish you a safe and happy holiday.
